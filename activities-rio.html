Surf INN Experiences

Waves. Arts. Culture. Spirit.

From salty mornings to golden sunsets, every day with Surf Inn flows with rhythm, nature, and deep connection.

Surf and Yoga lessons everyday and much more in the city of Rio

Surf Lessons in Rio de Janeiro

Beginner to advanced surf coaching

Improve your surfing with English speaking coaches and waves for all levels in our Surf School. Our surfing lessons happens everyday with duration of 1.5 - 2 hours in small groups (max 3 students per coach) or private sessions. No experience needed. All gear included.

Book your lesson

Yoga Lessons

Our yoga sessions are designed to stretch, restore, and reconnect — whether you're a first-timer or a dedicated yogi. Held in our gym or at the beach, our Hatha or Vinyasa sessions are the perfect way to start or end your day in Rio.

Ayurvedic & Shiatsu Massages

Recover from your surf sessions with our holistic massages. Choose Ayurvedic for a soothing oil massage or Shiatsu for deep tissue pressure to relieve tension and restore energy.

Surfskate Training - Train Your Surf on Land

Surfskate is the perfect tool to refine your posture, turns, and flow between sessions. Great for who is looking to accelerate progress.

Video Surf Analysis

Visual feedback for rapid improvement

Our video analysis helps you evolve session by session. We film your waves and review them with you to sharpen technique and boost confidence in the water.

The Carioca Experience

Become a Carioca / Live a Day Like a Local

Experience a day trip in the life of a Carioca. Start with a Surf lesson at Macumba Beach, enjoy a beachside organic acai, then choose between Football Game, Carnival Percussion Lesson.

Rio City Tour

Visit Rio's top attractions in a single tour: Christ the Redeemer, Sugar Loaf, and Escadaria Selaron. The main attractions of the city.

Guided Hikes in Rio

Join us on Hikes like the Telegrafo Rock, Stand-up Paddling at the Mangrove, or the hidden Waterfalls in the Atlantic Forest — led by locals who share the stories behind each path.
